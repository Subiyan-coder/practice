Asta

He is a short-statured orphan raised at Hage church after being abandoned by his mother, Richita, shortly after birth. Unlike everyone else in the Clover Kingdom, who are born with magical ability, Asta is born without any mana, making him unable to use magic. Despite this, he possesses a unique ability called Anti-Magic, which allows him to nullify and repel magical energy.

Gon & Killua

Gon, a young boy from Whale Island, embarks on a journey to become a Hunter to find his father, Ging Freecss, who abandoned him as a child. Killua, the middle child of the infamous Zoldyck assassin family, joins Gon after meeting him during the Hunter Exam, seeking freedom from his family's oppressive life. Their friendship begins when they are the only two participants of the same age during the exam, and it quickly becomes a profound bond.

Natsu

Natsu Dragneel is a Dragon Slayer wizard from the Fairy Tail Guild, raised by the dragon Igneel. He is known for his fiery personality, love for adventure, and strong sense of justice. Natsu's primary goal is to find Igneel, who disappeared seven years prior to the series' events, and he often embarks on quests with his friends Lucy Heartfilia and Happy.
